Who we are

We are Youths with green ideas and burning desire for a transformed Society. Our hope is to see to it that Any African Youth achieve his/her her goals in life.

Mision

Initiate Programmes to harness hidden potentials in young Africans. Raise Youths with green ideas as captains of industries,Promote Youths inclusiveness in governance.

Vision

To integrate 200 million Young Africans for continental action on community development through youth empowerment and young leadership development before the end of 2030.

Goals

At the core of the 2030 goals of the community is the integration of 200 million African youths around the globe for continental action in developing immediate communities.

Fuel your success

Africa's greatest resource is its youth. Yet, this abundant resource has been relatively untapped and facing cross-cutting issues such as limited access to education and finances, deterring health, scarce employment opportunities and low wages, and often excluded from local decision-making.

Be involvement

Ideas capable of reshaping our narratives and lives often spring up from the most unexpected of places. The first move towards the reshaping of these narratives is boosting the creative industry which uses music, fashion, art, dance to convey stories through creative and imaginative ways that easily appeal to the youth who make up 60% of the African population.
