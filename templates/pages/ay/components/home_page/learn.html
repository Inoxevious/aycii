Who we are

We are Youths with green ideas and burning desire for a transformed Society. Our hope is to see to it that Any African Youth achieve his/her her goals in life.

Mision

Initiate Programmes to harness hidden potentials in young Africans. Raise Youths with green ideas as captains of industries,Promote Youths inclusiveness in governance.

Vision

To integrate 200 million Young Africans for continental action on community development through youth empowerment and young leadership development before the end of 2030.

Goals

At the core of the 2030 goals of the community is the integration of 200 million African youths around the globe for continental action in developing immediate communities.
